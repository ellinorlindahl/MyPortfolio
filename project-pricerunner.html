PriceRunner | Internship | 2021

Mission: A 9 week in-depth evaluation and testing of Pricerunner's digital workspace / internal operations system, from a UX perspective.

      Methods used:
  • Heuristic analysis
  • Benchmarking
  • User surveys
  • User interviews
  • Cognitive walkthrough
  • Open card sorting
  • An iterative ideation process

Result: A thorough report and presentation of our research findings, along with well-recieved suggestions for future development of the service.
